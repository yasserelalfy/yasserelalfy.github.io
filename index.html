Research — Overview

I focus on learning-based perception and mapping for autonomy. I like work that is measurable, reproducible, and grounded in real constraints.

Core strengths

  • Experiment design, evaluation, and reproducibility
  • Modeling & analysis (Python, deep learning tooling)
  • Prototype building for research-to-product translation

About

I’m a researcher focused on rigorous empirical work: experiment design, measurement, and reproducible pipelines. I enjoy translating research into practical systems and tools.

Skills: Python, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Evaluation, Reproducibility
